Reflective Journal - What I Learned in PPW

Week 1: Business Writing

This week I learned the fundamentals of business writing. I was surprised by how important clear and concise communication is in a professional setting. Understanding the audience and purpose of each piece of writing was a key takeaway.

Week 2: Formal Email Writing

This week I learned the structure and tone required for formal email communication. I practiced writing emails for different scenarios, focusing on clarity and professionalism. Understanding the importance of subject lines and concise messaging was a key takeaway.

Week 3: Memo Writing

This week I learned the structure and purpose of memos in a business context. I practiced writing concise memos that clearly communicated key information and action items. Understanding the importance of a clear subject line and a professional tone was a key takeaway.

Week 4: Presentation Skills

This week I learned how to create effective presentations. I focused on structuring my content logically, using visuals to enhance understanding, and practicing my delivery to engage the audience. Understanding the importance of body language and voice modulation was a key takeaway.

Week 5: Formal Letter Writing

This week I learned the structure and tone required for formal letter writing. I practiced writing letters for different scenarios, focusing on clarity and professionalism. Understanding the importance of salutations, closings, and concise messaging was a key takeaway.

Week 6: How to Face an Interview

This week I learned how to prepare for job interviews. I focused on researching the company, understanding the job description, and practicing common interview questions. Understanding the importance of body language and making a good first impression was a key takeaway.

Week 7: Telephone Etiquette

This week I learned about professional manners over phone calls. I focused on how to handle difficult conversations, take notes, and actively listen. Understanding the importance of tone and clarity in verbal communication was a key takeaway.

Week 8: Effective Listening Skills

This week I learned how to improve my listening skills in professional settings. I focused on techniques for active listening, such as maintaining eye contact, nodding, and summarizing what the speaker has said. Understanding the importance of empathy and asking clarifying questions was a key takeaway.

Week 9: Effective Speaking Skills

This week I learned how to improve my speaking skills in professional settings. I focused on techniques for clear and confident speech, such as organizing my thoughts, using appropriate language, and engaging my audience. Understanding the importance of tone and pacing was a key takeaway.

Week 10: Grammar Focus

This week I learned about the importance of grammar in professional communication. I focused on common grammatical errors, sentence structure, and the use of formal language. Understanding the impact of grammar on clarity and professionalism was a key takeaway.

Additional Learnings from PPW Course

In the Preparation for the Professional World (PPW) course, I developed numerous professional skills that complement my technical knowledge. I learned about:

  • Business & Formal Communication: Writing effective business emails, memos, formal and informal letters with proper tone, structure, and language.
  • Presentation Skills: How to structure presentations, engage the audience, use visual aids effectively, and manage stage presence and voice.
  • Interview Preparation: Strategies for common interview questions, using the STAR method for behavioral questions, body language tips, and virtual vs. in-person interview techniques.
  • Telephone Etiquette: Professional manners over phone calls, including how to handle difficult conversations, take notes, and actively listen.
  • Interpersonal Communication: How to communicate effectively in teams, understand feedback, and resolve conflicts.
  • Report Writing: Planning, drafting, structuring, and presenting professional reports, and creating concise and reader-friendly documents.

This course emphasized active listening, empathy, professionalism, and clarity in all workplace interactions, which will undoubtedly help me in future roles.
